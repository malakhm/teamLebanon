About our founder

Ramy Atieh is a 30-year-old professional dancer with over a decade of experience in the dance industry. He currently serves as the head of freestyle at the Dance Federation of Lebanon. Throughout his career, Ramy has participated in and won numerous prestigious competitions, including first place at the International Hip-Hop Championship (2012) and Illskill All Styles (2017).

Beyond his competitive success, Ramy has played a key role in the organization and judging of several dance battles and cultural events. His notable performances include appearances at the Jbeil Festival, Forum de Beyrouth Sports Event, and Downtown Sports Activities. For the past two years, he has led a dedicated dance team focused on conducting workshops, curating performances, and producing major events—most recently, the Game Event 2023 and 2025.
