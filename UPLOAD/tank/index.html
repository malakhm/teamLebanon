About us

We are a dynamic and versatile dance crew based in Lebanon. Our team brings together a powerful blend of movement arts, offering a wide range of talents including dance styles such as hip-hop, breakdance, commercial, street jazz, and contemporary, as well as circus performances like aerial hoop, aerial silk, and fire shows, in addition to theatrical expression.

Our work spans live shows, entertainment events, music videos, advertisements, and more. We are also passionate about the dance community, regularly organizing multi-style dance battles and creative collaborations that celebrate movement and artistic expression.
